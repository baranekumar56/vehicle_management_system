FAQ's

+ How often should I book a regular service?
Most vehicles should be serviced every 12 months or 10,000–15,000 km, depending on make, model, and usage. Always follow your manufacturer's recommendations for optimal performance and longevity.
+ Can I track my vehicle’s service status?
Yes, Auto Care lets customers track their service status in real-time through the platform, so updates are always just a click away.
+ Does Auto Care maintain a history of my past services?
Absolutely! All your past services, repairs, and appointments are available in your Auto Care profile for your convenience and future reference.
+ Can I reschedule or cancel a service appointment?
Yes, appointments can be rescheduled or canceled right from your dashboard, as long as it is prior to the scheduled service time.
+ Are the prices fixed or only given as estimates?
Auto Care provides a clear estimate before booking; most routine services have fixed prices, but some repairs may require a physical inspection for an accurate quote.
+ Will my warranty be affected if I use Auto Care?
All services and repairs at Auto Care adhere to manufacturer guidelines, so your car’s warranty is protected as long as services are done on schedule.
+ What if I need to drop off or pick up my car after hours?
Auto Care offers after-hours drop-off and pick-up arrangements for customers with busy schedules—just contact support to coordinate convenient timings.
